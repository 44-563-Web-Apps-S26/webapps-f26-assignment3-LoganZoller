How to Make a Grilled Cheese

  1. Get supplies
    1. bread
    2. butter
    3. cheese
    4. knife
    5. pan
  2. Assemble
    1. butter both pieces of bread
    2. put cheese in between the pieces of bread
    3. turn stove on
  3. Cook
    1. When pan is hot, place sandwich on pan
    2. When the first piece of bread is toasted, flip
    3. Once the second piece of bread is toasted and cheese is melted, remove
    4. eat and enjoy grilled cheese
